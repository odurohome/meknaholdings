Our Story

Building Wealth Through Strategic Real Estate

Mekna Holdings Inc. is a two-division company dedicated to excellence in real estate and business technology.

Our Real Estate division focuses on value-add investing. We acquire properties directly from homeowners and renovate them with care and craftsmanship to create lasting value. Whether serving luxury or budget-friendly markets, we offer upgraded homes for sale or rent to both individuals and businesses, all guided by our core principles of integrity, stewardship, and faith

Complementing our real estate work, our Business Technology division provides software and AI-powered solutions designed specifically for realtors and brokerages. Our tools enhance marketing, lead capture, and client communication, allowing teams to automate workflows, stay organized, and close more deals while providing a superior client experience.
